YOUR WORKOUT PLAN

3-DAY FULL BODY // BEGINNER // GYM-BASED // PROGRESSIVE OVERLOAD

The Goal: At 6'4" and 235 lbs, you have a great athletic frame. This plan focuses on compound movements (big multi-muscle exercises) that burn the most calories, build functional muscle, and spark your metabolism ‚Äî all in 3 days a week. Abs are built in the kitchen AND the gym. You'll hit core every session.
